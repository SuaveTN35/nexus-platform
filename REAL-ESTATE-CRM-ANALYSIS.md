## ğŸ¯ Key Insights for AEQUALIS Platform

### 1. **Essential Real Estate Features**
All top CRMs include:
- **Lead Management**: Core to real estate operations
- **Client Management**: Contact and relationship tracking
- **Task Management**: Follow-ups and workflows
- **Analytics**: Performance tracking and reporting
- **Property/Inventory Management**: Listing and property data

### 2. **Tech Stack Patterns**
- **Frontend**: React.js is dominant (modern, component-based)
- **Backend**: Node.js/Express or Rails (both RESTful)
- **Database**: MongoDB (flexible schema) or PostgreSQL (relational)
- **UI Libraries**: Material UI, Tailwind CSS, or custom

### 3. **Architecture Patterns**
- **Monolithic vs Microservices**: Most are monolithic (simpler for MVP)
- **API-First**: RESTful APIs for frontend-backend separation
- **Component-Based**: React components for reusability
- **Role-Based Access**: Essential for multi-user systems
